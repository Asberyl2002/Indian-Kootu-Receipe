About Us

HI, WELCOME TO Simple Kootu Recipes!

A cozy space for traditional South Indian comfort food.

Simple Kootu Recipes is dedicated to sharing easy, healthy, and authentic kootu dishes from South Indian kitchens. Kootu is a wholesome blend of lentils and vegetables, lightly spiced and packed with nutrition.

This site was created to help beginners and home cooks prepare delicious everyday meals without complexity. Whether you're cooking for your family or exploring Indian cuisine, you'll find recipes that are simple, quick, and full of flavor.

From classic vegetable kootu to regional variations, each recipe is explained step-by-step with helpful tips to make your cooking easier.
